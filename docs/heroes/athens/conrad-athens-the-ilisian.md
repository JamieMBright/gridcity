## Description
hotel in Athens, Greece

Conrad Athens The Ilisian is a redevelopment of the historic Athens Hilton hotel. It is located in Athens, Greece, on Vassilissis Sofias Avenue, within the Hilton Area, adjacent to the Kolonaki and Pangrati neighbourhoods. Opened in 1963, it closed for renovations in January 2022 and re-opened in April 2026 where it houses the Conrad Athens The Ilisian, and new luxury residences under the Conrad and Waldorf Astoria brands on the top floors of the building.
