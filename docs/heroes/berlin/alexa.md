## Description
shopping center in Berlin, Germany

Alexa Centre or simply Alexa, is a shopping centre near Alexanderplatz in Berlin, Germany. With a rental area of 56,200 m2 (605,000 sq ft), it was the second-largest shopping centre in Berlin at the time of its opening, after Gropius Passagen, but Alexa was the largest in terms of number of shops. Over one million people visit the Alexa Centre per month.
