## Description
architectural structure

The Bikini-Berlin complex, formerly Centre at the Zoo, is a building complex on Budapest street in the Charlottenburg locality of west Berlin. Bikini Berlin includes the Bikini-Haus shopping centre, the Huthmacher-Haus, the Zoo Palast, the Kaiser Wilhelm Memorial Church, a multi-storey car park, and the 25hours Hotel. Every building in the complex is a listed building.
