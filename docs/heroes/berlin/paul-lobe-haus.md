## Description
building in Berlin, Germany

The Paul Löbe House is an office building of the German Bundestag in Berlin's government district. It is located on the site of the former Alsenviertel district on the southern edge of Spreebogenpark. It is named after Paul Löbe, a Social Democratic Party (SPD) politician, who was President of the Reichstag and oldest member of the first German Bundestag. The main entrance is on Konrad-Adenauer-Straße, which runs to the west. Another entrance is on Paul-Löbe-Allee.
