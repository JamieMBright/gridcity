## Description
mosque in Egypt

The Mosque-Sabil of Sulayman Agha al-Silahdar, also known as the Mosque-Sabil-Kuttab of Sulayman Agha al-Silahdar, is an Islamic religious complex that contains a mosque, sabil, and kuttab, located at the beginning of Burjouan alley of the famed Muizz Street, in Islamic Cairo, Egypt. The complex was established in 1839 CE, during the era of Muhammad Ali Pasha. On the other side it faces the extension of Al-Nahasin Street leading to the Bab Al-Shaareya square.
