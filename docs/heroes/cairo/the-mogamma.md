## Description
administrative building in Cairo, Egypt

 Mogamma el Tahrir is a government building in Cairo, Egypt. The Mogamma was the result of a series of master plans for Esamailiyya Square, which used to be occupied by the British barracks. In 1945 when King Farouk ordered the demolition of the barracks upon the departure of British troops from the area, a series of urban planning proposals ensued.
