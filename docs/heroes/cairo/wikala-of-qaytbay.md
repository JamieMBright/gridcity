## Description
caravanserai, apartment complex in Cairo, Egypt

The Wikala of Sultan Qaytbay or Wakala of Sultan Qaytbay is an urban caravanserai and rab built by Sultan al-Ashraf Abu al-Nasr Qaitbay in 1481 CE in Islamic Cairo, the historic medieval district of Cairo, Egypt. It is located just east of and adjacent to Al-Hakim Mosque.
