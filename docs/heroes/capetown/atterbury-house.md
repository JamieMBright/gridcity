## Description
office skyscraper in Cape Town, South Africa

Atterbury House is a 417 ft (127 m) office skyscraper in Cape Town, South Africa. The 29 story building was completed in 1976. At the time it was completed, it became the 2nd tallest building in Cape Town, shorter than the 423 ft (129 m) 1 Thibault Square. It is now the city's 5th tallest building.
