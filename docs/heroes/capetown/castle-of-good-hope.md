## Description
17th-century bastion fort in Cape Town, South African

The Castle of Good Hope is a 17th century bastion fort in Cape Town, South Africa. Originally located on the coastline of Table Bay, following land reclamation the fort is now located inland. In 1936 the Castle was declared a historical monument and following restorations in the 1980's it is considered the best preserved example of a Dutch East India Company fort.
