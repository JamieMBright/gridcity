## Description
building in South Africa

The Media24 Centre is a 26-storey, 93-metre (305 ft) skyscraper located at 40 Heerengracht Street in the Foreshore area of Cape Town, South Africa. As of 2026, it is the 14th-tallest building in Cape Town, and forms a prominent part of the Cape Town CBD skyline.
