## Description
superior court of law in the Western Cape, South Africa

The Western Cape Division of the High Court of South Africa is a superior court of law with general jurisdiction over the Western Cape province of South Africa. The division, which sits at Cape Town, consists of 30 judges led by Judge President NP Mabindla-Boqwana.
