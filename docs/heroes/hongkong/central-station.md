## Description
MTR metro station

Central is an MTR station located in the Central area of Hong Kong Island. The station's livery is firebrick red but dark brown on the Tsuen Wan line platforms. The station is the southern terminus of the Tsuen Wan line, a stop on the Island line, and connects to Hong Kong station, which serves the Tung Chung line and the Airport Express.
