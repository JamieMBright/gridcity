## Description
building under construction in London

Platform 37 is an office building under construction in King's Cross, London, forming part of the Knowledge Quarter in King's Cross Central. It will be the headquarters of Google in the United Kingdom. It is the first building wholly owned and designed by Google outside the United States. Platform 37 features "terraced gardens on the roof."
