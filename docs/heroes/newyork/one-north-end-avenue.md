## Description
Office building in Manhattan, New York

One North End Avenue, also known as the New York Mercantile Exchange Building, is an office building at the Brookfield Place complex in Lower Manhattan, New York City. It is located on the coast of Battery Park City and the Hudson River and in front of 250 Vesey Street. It serves as the headquarters and trading facility of the New York Mercantile Exchange.
