## Description
two-building residential complex

The Greenpoint is a two-building residential complex in the Greenpoint neighborhood of Brooklyn. The two buildings are a 400 feet (120 m) tower with condos and a block-long five-story building with rentals. It is located next to the Greenpoint ferry stop.
