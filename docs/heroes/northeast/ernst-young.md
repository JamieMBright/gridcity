## Description
multinational professional services network based in England

EY, previously known as Ernst & Young, is a British multinational professional services network based in London, England. Along with Deloitte, KPMG and PwC, it is one of the Big Four professional services firms. The EY network is composed of member firms of Ernst & Young Global Limited, a UK company limited by guarantee.
