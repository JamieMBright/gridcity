## Description
bus station in Newcastle upon Tyne

Haymarket bus station is a bus station located in the Haymarket area of Newcastle upon Tyne. It is situated on Percy Street, adjacent to Haymarket Metro. It is a short walk from both Newcastle and Northumbria Universities, Newcastle Civic Centre and the Great North Museum: Hancock.
