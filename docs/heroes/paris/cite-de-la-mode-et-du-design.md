## Description
building in 13th arrondissement of Paris, France

The Cité de la mode et du design is a building located at the site of the old general storehouses on the Quai d'Austerlitz in the 13th arrondissement of Paris, France. The public opening, originally scheduled for early 2008, took place in 2010.
