## Description
luxury department store in the 7ᵗʰ arrondissement of Paris, France

Le Bon Marché is a department store in the 7th arrondissement of Paris, France. Founded in 1838 and revamped almost completely by Aristide Boucicaut in 1852, it was one of the first modern department stores. It was a member of the International Association of Department Stores from 1986 to 2011.
