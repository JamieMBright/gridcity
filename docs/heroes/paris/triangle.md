## Description
proposed skyscraper in Paris, France

The Tour Triangle is an under-construction skyscraper at the Parc des Expositions de la Porte de Versailles in Paris's Rive Gauche, France. Designed by the Swiss firm Herzog & de Meuron, it takes the shape of a 180 m (590 ft) tall glass pyramid with trapezoid base, wide from one side and narrow from another.
