# COEP Boat Club
*boat club, riverside · 1928*

Reference: real Pune landmark (research compiled for the bespoke-hero sprite).
Wikidata/Wikipedia: search "COEP Boat Club Pune".

## Description
The historic rowing club of the College of Engineering Pune on the Mula-Mutha, a low colonial timber-and-stone pavilion with a deep pitched veranda roof, a flag and moored rowing sculls; one of the oldest collegiate boat clubs in India, beside the Sangam.
