# Empress Botanical Garden
*garden, glasshouse · 1838*

## Description
A 39-acre botanical garden in the Camp, dating to 1838 and named for Queen Victoria as Empress of India. Old rain-trees and rare plants, lawns, a Ganesh temple and Victorian glasshouse/garden structures; one of the oldest gardens in the city.
