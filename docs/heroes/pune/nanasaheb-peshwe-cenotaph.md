## Description
A memorial cenotaph (chhatri/samadhi) to a Peshwa of the Maratha state, a small white domed memorial chamber on a stepped plinth with cusped niches, a kalash finial and a tulsi-vrindavan; one of the riverside Peshwa-era memorials of old Pune.
