## Description
A historic school founded in 1880 by Lokmanya Tilak, Agarkar and Chiplunkar in Tilak Road — the seed institution of the Deccan Education Society. A long heritage schoolhouse with verandah arcades, a clock-and-bell gable and a tiled roof.
