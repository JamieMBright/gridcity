## Description
An earthfill dam on the Ambi river in the Western Ghats south-west of Pune, holding the Tanajisagar reservoir; infamous for its 1961 breach that flooded Pune, since rebuilt. A long dam wall holding a sheet of water amid green hills; a scenic reservoir and watersports spot.
