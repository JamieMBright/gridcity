## Description
A historic colonial gymkhana club founded in 1882 in the Pune Cantonment, set in extensive grounds with a long low clubhouse: deep pitched Mangalore-tile roofs, pillared verandahs, dormers and chimneys, surrounded by sporting greens and old rain-trees.
