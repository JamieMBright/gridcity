## Description
metro station in Pune, India

Pune Railway Station is an important elevated metro station on the east–west corridor of the Aqua Line of Pune Metro in Pune, India, which holds the main Pune Junction railway station. The station was opened on 1 August 2023 as an extension of Pune Metro Phase I. Aqua Line operates between Vanaz and Ramwadi.
