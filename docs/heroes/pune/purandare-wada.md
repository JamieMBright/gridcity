# Purandare Wada
*wada (mansion) · 1750*

Reference: real Pune landmark (research compiled for the bespoke-hero sprite).
Wikidata/Wikipedia: search "Purandare Wada Pune".

## Description
The Pune residence of the Purandare family, hereditary diwans of the Peshwas. A substantial courtyard wada of stone and carved teak with first-floor balconies, brackets and a tiled roof, typical of the great administrative families of 18th-century Pune.
