# Raste Wada
*wada (mansion) · 1765*

Reference: real Pune landmark (research compiled for the bespoke-hero sprite).
Wikidata/Wikipedia: search "Raste Wada Pune".

## Description
The mansion of the Raste (Rastia) family, prominent Peshwa-era sardars, in Rasta Peth — a quarter named after them. A large courtyard wada with carved teak galleries, a fortified gateway and tiled roofs; the family also endowed temples and the city's water supply.
