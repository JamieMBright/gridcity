## Description
building in Shanghai, China

The Longemont Shanghai is a skyscraper and mixed use building in the Changning District of Shanghai, China. It contains the Changfeng Hotel as well as office space. It is 218 metres (715 ft) high, has 53 stories and was completed in 2005.
