## Description
residential apartment building in Sydney, Australia

Century Tower is a residential apartment building in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. Designed by Brewster Murray, it was completed in 1997 and held the title of Australia's tallest residential building until 2002 with a total height of 183 metres (600 ft). It is located on 343–357 Pitt Street, between Wilmot Street and Central Street in Sydney's CBD.
