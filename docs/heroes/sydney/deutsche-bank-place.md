## Description
skyscraper in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia

Deutsche Bank Place is a 240-metre-high (790 ft), 39-storey skyscraper in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It is located at 126 Phillip Street in the north-eastern end of the central business district, across the road from Chifley Tower. Construction began in 2002 and was completed in 2005. The building's architect is Norman Foster of Foster + Partners.
