## Description
residential skyscraper in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia

The Greenland Centre is a 68-storey residential skyscraper in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia, located on the corner of Bathurst and Pitt Street. At a height of 237 m (778 ft), it is the seventh-tallest building in Sydney as of 2024.
