## Description
power station in New South Wales, Australia

The White Bay Power Station is a heritage listed former coal-fired power station on a 38,000 m2 (410,000 ft2) site in White Bay, in the suburb of Rozelle, 3 km (2 mi) from Sydney in New South Wales, Australia.
